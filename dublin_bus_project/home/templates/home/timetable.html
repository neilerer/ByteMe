    A big welcome to our journey predicting web app from Team ByteMe.

    We are happy you chose us, and only hope that after using our service you feel the same way. Team ByteMe is made up of Cara, Conor, Neil, and Otto, and we started out on this project two long months ago.
    Our main objective in building this app, was to put the commuter first. We drew upon our past experiences as commuters ourselves, and asked ourselves the question, "what would make our commuting experience better?"
    The answer to that question is what you see before you in the form of this intuitive and informative app.
    We hope you have as much fun using it as we did building it.
    Team ByteMe
